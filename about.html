Mortgages
We offer a variety of mortgages to meet the needs of most of our customers.

Fixed Rate
These are ideal for people who want certainty on month on month basis of the amount that they’re going to have to repay for the next few years. As a result you have a choice usually of how long you can fix your mortgage. This ranges from 2, 3 or 5 years. During this fixed period your payments will remain the same every month. After the expiry of your fixed period your payments will calculated based on Standard Variable rate. When you move to a new home you can carry your fixed rate mortgage to your new home depending on its value. What is more If you wish to repay your mortgage in the fixed period you can do so however you may need to pay an early repayment charge.

Tracker Rate
These track a particular rate such as the Bank Of England’s Bank Rate. These are suitable for people who believe rates will remain low for a while or those who want to make extra repayments on their mortgages. People who think interest rates will stay low over the next few years or want to make unlimited overpayments on their mortgage. You can choose a mortgage with an initial rate period and during this period your rate tracks above the Bank of England base rate. On these mortgages the payments may vary depending on the changes in the tracker. The initial rate period is usually 3 years and after which the mortgage moves onto the Standard Variable Rate.

Lifetime Tracker
If you are sure that you will not want a fixed rate and you like the ability to repay whenever you have same extra money then the Lifetime tracker could be the one for you. There is no need to decide what type of mortgage you want every few years as it continues to Track the Tracking Index until you choose a different type of mortgage.
